

# Shepherd's Pie


## Requirements

### Ingredients

| Quantity | Unit | Ingredient                       |
|----------|------|----------------------------------|
| 1        | pkg  | lean (or extra lean) ground beef |
| ~        | pcs  | shallots (alt: onion)            |
| ~        | pcs  | garlic                           |
| 1        | can  | tomato paste                     |
| 1        | ctn  | beef broth                       |
| ~        | ~    | cheese                           |
| ~        | ~    | mashed potatoes                  |


## Instructions

- brown the shallots, add the garlic, brown the beef, add the tomato paste and half the carton of broth
- simmer
